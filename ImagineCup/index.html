ABOUT

Imagine you could do anything.

It all began with this. Take a moment to really think about it. Maybe you want to be a superhero. Maybe you want to be rich. I’ll tell you what. You will be both. Just follow me for a second…


Me? A rich superhero?

Yes, it’s YOU I’m talking about. But how? Well… that would be by becoming a leader. The answer might surprise you but leadership is all about being someone’s hero, inspiring people, helping them evolve and become rich at the same time. But I don’t mean money here. You get rich by gaining experience, meeting new people, making new contacts and finding out about your true passion, pursuing it while taking other people along with you on your road.

This is what we are offering you: four fun days, experiencing leadership with the help of professional speakers and then warming up during breaks with a cup of coffee because November is so cold. It would be the experience of your lifetime, if you chose to take it.

Maybe we weren’t clear enough for you. Please send us your questions to the email address il.team@yahoo.com which is also found in the Contact page. Or give a call to one of the people listed there.

Trust us. We are eager to answer all of your questions.
